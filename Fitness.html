Full Body Workout for Everyone 💪

Boost your energy and strengthen your body with this beginner-friendly routine.

1. Jumping Jacks

  1. Stand tall with feet together and arms at your sides.
  2. Jump while spreading your legs and raising your arms overhead.
  3. Jump back to starting position and repeat for 30–60 seconds.

2. Squats

  1. Keep your feet shoulder-width apart.
  2. Lower your hips as if sitting into a chair.
  3. Keep your chest up and knees behind toes.
  4. Return to standing position. Repeat 10–15 times.

3. Push-ups (Modified or Full)

  1. Place hands under shoulders and extend legs back.
  2. Lower your body until your chest nearly touches the ground.
  3. Push back up. Use knees for modified push-ups.
  4. Repeat 8–12 times.

4. Plank

  1. Rest on your forearms with your body straight from head to heels.
  2. Engage your core and hold the position.
  3. Start with 20 seconds and build up to 60 seconds.

5. Arm Circles

  1. Extend arms out to your sides at shoulder height.
  2. Make small circles forward for 30 seconds.
  3. Reverse and circle backward for another 30 seconds.
